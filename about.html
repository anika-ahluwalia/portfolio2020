About Me
My name is Anika Ahluwalia

I am a sophomore studying computer science with a focus on design and data science at Brown University. I am passionate about accessible education for all across the globe and hope to be able to use my skills to contribute to global solutions in this area.

Right now, I work as a Research Developer in the Pen and Touch Computing Group at Brown University on development of the Dash Software, a hypermedia platform aimed at streamlining workflows for academics. Next summer I will be joining Google as a STEP Intern.

Outside of work, I love to play and watch tennis and am an avid baker. I also love carefully curating Spotify playlists and watching any and every movie I can find.
